A TimeService that simulates time and allows for fine-grained control over advancing time and waking up sleeping tasks.

Auto Advance

MockTimeService has the option to auto advance time when new Sleeps are crated. When weâ€™re auto advancing, new Sleeps are immediately resolved and the simulation time is advanced to the wait deadline. New Sleeps whose deadlines are after the auto advance deadline will be pending and must be manually woken by the MockTimeService::advance_{..} methods.

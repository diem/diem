A wrapper around a tokio TcpStream

In order to properly implement the AsyncRead/AsyncWrite traits we need to wrap a TcpStream to ensure that the "close" method actually closes the write half of the TcpStream. This is because the "close" method on a TcpStream just performs a no-op instead of actually shutting down the write side of the TcpStream.
